Deep Space

Deep Space is an Android space adventure game. My team and I built Deep Space at Mission Bit San Francisco's Android Game Development course, using Android Studio Code and the libGDX game development library. The player controls a spaceship that is speeding through space with the help of a solar sail. They will swipe left/right/up/down to avoid obstacles such as planets, stars, comets, or asteroids. The celestial objects they pass will be labeled with their names. They will collect photons along the way to push their solar sail and keep them moving. The game is over when you hit an obstacle. Your final score will be how long your journey lasted, which we will record with a timer.
