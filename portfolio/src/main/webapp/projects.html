Deep Space

Deep Space is an Android space obstacle game. My team and I created this at Mission Bit's Android Game Development course, using Android Studio Code and the libGDX game development library. The player controls a spaceship that is speeding through space with the help of a solar sail. They will swipe left/right/up/down to avoid obstacles such as planets, stars, comets, or asteroids. The celestial objects they pass will be labeled with their names, and we may also add in a feature that affects the movement of the spaceship based on the obstacleâ€™s gravitational field. They will collect photons along the way to push their solar sail and keep them moving. The game is over when you hit an obstacle. Your final score will be how long your journey lasted, which we will record with a timer.
